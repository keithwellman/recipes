1 red onion
1.5 lb ground turkey
1/3 cup sun-dried tomatoes in oil, drained and chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 Tbsp Greek seasoning
1/2 tsp salt
1 (4-oz) pkg crumbled feta cheese, divided 
2 cups baby arugula (or any lettuce or baby spinach)
2 large roma tomatoes, sliced
6 burger buns (any type), toasted
mayonnaise

Instructions:
1. Cut red onion into thin rings; finely chop enough onion to yield 1/3 cup, and set aside remaining sliced onion as a topping.
2. Combine ground turkey, 1/3 cup onion, sun-dried tomatoes, garlic, Greek seasoning and salt; mix with hands. Stir in 1/4 cup feta cheese. Shape mixture into 6 patties.
3. Cook burgers in a large skillet (or grill pan) over medium-heat 4 to 5 minutes per side or until no longer pink. Assemble sandwiches with mayonnaise, burger patties, sliced tomato, red onion rings, arugula, and remaining feta cheese on toasted buns.
